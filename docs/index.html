CAMERA SETTINGS: LIGHTING

Aperture:

The opening in a lens through which light passes to enter the camera and is measured in 'F-stop'.

ISO:

How sensitive the sensor/film is to light. ISO is its' own measurement.

Shutter Speed:

How fast the shutter closes and is measured in seconds.
